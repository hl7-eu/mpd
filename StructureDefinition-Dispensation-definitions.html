0. Dispensation
Definition

Dispensation information (provided by the dispensing pharmacy). Section B1 of the guidelines

ShortB.1 Dispensation information (provided by the dispensing pharmacy)
Control0..*
Is Modifierfalse
Logical ModelInstances of this logical model are not marked to be the target of a Reference
2. Dispensation.dispenserIdentifier
Definition

A unique number or code issued for the purpose of identifying a dispenser [ISO/TS 27527:2010]; this may be a licence or registration number which can be used to trace the dispenser and to check whether a medicinal product was dispensed by the right person according to the law of the dispensing country.

ShortB.1.1 Identifier of the dispenser
Control0..*
TypeIdentifier
4. Dispensation.dispenserFamilyName
Definition

The family name/surname/last name of the dispenser. This enables the dispenser to be traced in the event of questions or emergencies.

ShortB.1.2 Family name of the dispenser
Control0..*
Typestring
Primitive ValueThis primitive element may be present, or absent, or replaced by an extension
6. Dispensation.dispenserGivenName
Definition

The given name/first name of the dispenser. This enables the dispenser to be traced in the event of questions or emergencies.

ShortB.1.3 Given name of the dispenser

Definition

Details of direct contact could be an email address and/or phone/fax number of the dispensing pharmacy.

As described in A.1.2.

ShortB.1.7 Identifier of the prescription
Control0..*
TypeIdentifier
16. Dispensation.medicinalProduct
Definition

Information about the dispensed product as described in A.1.4. The cardinality of the data elements in the product description may differ between ePrescription and eDispensation.

ShortB.1.8 Medicinal product
Control0..*
TypeBase
18. Dispensation.dispensedQuantity
Definition

The package size should be clear from the medicinal product data and the overall amount should be automatically calculable. The dispensed quantity may differ from the prescribed quantity.

  • Preferred system(s): UCUM, EDQM
ShortB.1.9 Dispensed quantity
Control0..*
TypeQuantity
