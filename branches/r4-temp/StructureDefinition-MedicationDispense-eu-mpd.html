Resource Profile: MedicationDispense: MPD

Official URL: http://hl7.eu/fhir/mpd/StructureDefinition/MedicationDispense-eu-mpd Version: 0.1.0
Draft as of 2025-05-18 Computable Name: MedicationDispenseEuMpd

Copyright/Legal: Used by permission of HL7 Europe, all rights reserved Creative Commons License

This profile defines how to represent the supply and the associated administration instructions in HL7 FHIR for the purpose of this guide.

Usage:

  • Examples for this Resource Profile: MedicationDispense/300D-dispense-for-2-requests, MedicationDispense/400D-dispense-1, MedicationDispense/400D-dispense-2 and MedicationDispense/400E-refused-dispense
